Design

Forget the glass from the past.

The GlassPad features a custom-designed, state of the art glass material which is shatterproof, waterproof, and fireproof while maintaining a smooth, sensitive surface to ensure a user-friendly experience. The glass is treated to be anti-glare, anti-fingerprint, and smudge-free so that users will enjoy a clean, smooth tablet at all times for easy readability, clear displays, and easy finger maneuverability. The tablet is also made with comfort in mind. The glass will maintain a steady, safe temperature at all times so nobody will wake up on a chilly morning with an ice-cold tablet. The glass is also textured to be softer than any other glass in existence.

Only the best will make the cut.

While other companies settle for subpar manufacturing, the GlassPad is created only through the finest means possible; meaning what goes into your hands is crafted to near perfection. The material itself is impressive, but coupled with our high tech cutting and manufacturing equipment (including a laser cutter that cuts so precisely and so deeply that the government has taken an interest), customers will love the seamless and professional look of their GlassPads and will find satisfaction in knowing that nothing but the best is used to create them.

On the go.

At only 0.15 inches, the GlassPad is thinner than almost any other mobile device out there. Combined with its durability and resistance to scratching and smudging, the tablet will easily travel anywhere, with anyone, at any time. Its lightweight design also adds to the easy movability, so feel free to chat with a friend, play a game, or read a book no matter where you are.
