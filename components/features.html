Features

Scan anything.

The see-through technology on the GlassPad makes it extremely easy to scan anything you could possibly want to scan. With high tech scanners to identify and access to a huge online database of information, feel free to scan to find directions to a building in the distance, identify an animal from its footprints, or just find fun facts about the local landmark.

Interact with other GlassWare.

The GlassPad includes a syncing feature that allows for interaction with other PRISM products, such as the GlassWall and the GlassPhone. By syncing, pictures, projects, even ongoing webcam conversations can easily be transferred between devices to make your day just that much easier.

Enhance your game experience.

Augment your reality by making sure you make the most of your gaming time! With the glass tablet, anything you see through the glass can be part of your game experience. Interact with your current environment by shooting a digital ball into the trashcan across the room, or catch butterflies in your garden with a net!
